How to change your profile name?

Welcome to discuss.playit.gg userX!
If you don’t want userX anymore, update your username. Here’s how

1. Click your profile in the top right and select “Summary”

2. Go to the “Preferences” tab

3. Click on the pen under “Username” to change your name
