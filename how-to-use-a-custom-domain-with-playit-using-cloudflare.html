How to use a custom domain with playit using Cloudflare

For this, to work you need a Cloudflare account with a registered website (means you already own a domain like ims-network.net)

Step One

Log Into your playit.gg account and copy your tunnel address with the port

Step Two

Log Into Your Cloudflare Dashboard, and navigate to DNS

Step Three

Create a new SRV record

Step Four

Now, follow this carefully :)
Paste the IP that you coped into the big box

After pasting the IP, remove the port and paste it into:

Step Five

Let's fill in the rest of the information:
Protocol - TCP (Minecraft java)

The Protocol might change depending on the game that you are configuring

Service - _minecraft

Weight - 0
